## Evaluation Rubric (Summary)

The template scores outputs across these dimensions:
- **Factual accuracy** — claims verifiable against source
- **Source grounding** — all assertions linked to retrievable evidence
- **Logical consistency** — no internal contradictions
- **Completeness** — all required fields present
- **Hallucination rate** — target < 1% (Apogee threshold)
