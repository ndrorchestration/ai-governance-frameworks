## Purpose

Implements Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) as a documented risk mitigation
strategy for LLM hallucination. Generates answers grounded in a verified
knowledge base, satisfying NIST AI RMF MANAGE 2.2 (hallucination risk treatment)
and ISO 42001 §8.4 (operational control).
