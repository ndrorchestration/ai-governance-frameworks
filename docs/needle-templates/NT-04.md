## Purpose

Provides a structured workflow for authoring, documenting, and operationalizing
an AI governance specification. Satisfies ISO 42001 §6.1 (AI risk identification
and treatment planning) and §9.1 (monitoring and measurement), and NIST AI RMF
GOVERN 1.7 (human oversight documentation). This template is the upstream
definition layer that NT-01 evaluates against.

## Governance Spec Output Structure

Each run of NT-04 produces a spec document containing:

```
1. Scope — which AI systems and use cases are governed
2. Risk register — identified AI risks with severity and likelihood
3. Treatment decisions — accepted, mitigated, or transferred risks
4. Human oversight procedures — who reviews what, at what frequency
5. Metrics and monitoring — how governance effectiveness is measured
6. Version and ratification — date, owner, approval chain
```
